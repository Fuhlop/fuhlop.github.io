Prelude

The year is 2003, the war against legendary monsters rages on. At the front's brink, Chicago stands helpless. The city's Mayor, pressured by increasing monster attacks, finally accepted my help. My research has produced undeniably true results. The culmination... the next step of my distant forefather, Frankesteins, research. True rebirth, my most beautiful creations, the resurrected Heroes Beowulf and Mulan. We are the only things that can protect this city from evil's onslaught.
